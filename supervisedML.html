    • Link: Project Slides
    • Pre-Reqs: "Introduction to Supervised Machine Learning" Main Slides
    • Suggested Length: 2 hours
    • Tech Dependencies: Google Chrome, Teachable Machines, Scratch
    • Key Concepts: Teachable Machines, Application of AI, Algorithm, Scratch
    • Group Reqs: None
    • Description: Students are introduced to the concept of classification. By exploring Google's Teachable Machine tool, students learn about supervised machine learning. Then students are asked to build a cat-dog classifier but are unknowingly given a biased dataset. When the classifier works better on cats than dogs, students have the opportunity to retrain their classifiers with their own new datasets.
