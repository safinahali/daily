    • Description: Students apply what they have learned so far by constructing an ethical matrix around the YouTube Recommender Algorithm. Based on this ethical matrix, students determine a goal (or "opinion") for their algorithm. Students then choose which datasets they will feed into their algorithm, and how they intend to mitigate bias in their algorithm.
